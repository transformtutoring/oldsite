What Makes Us Different?




We include college admissions counseling as a complimentary benefit with our academic & SAT tutoring.

As alumni of top universities, we know the admissions process is difficult. For example, each of the schools we graduated from accept less than 4%, 6%, and 12% of students respectively, each year.

Students are more likely to succeed with a proper admissions strategy -- counseling which is provided to all of our students as a part of our tutoring package.

Feel free to ask more about this, for more information.

Our tutors are excellent at reaching students who are struggling in class.

Students who are struggling in class now (for example, currently in a C or D grade range) often need a special type of tutoring, in which the teacher takes exquisite care to simplify advanced concepts into smaller, understandable ones.

Our tutors go through extensive, multi-hour trainings to achieve this level of teaching. For this reason, we’ve had phenomenal results with our students, particularly ones who’ve had a history of low grades or academic struggle.
